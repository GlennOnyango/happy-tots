Academics

At Happy Tots we focus on the primary educational development of a child by offering the first eight years of the 8-4-4 system. This is a widely accepted system in Kenya with many to almost all secondaries in the country being able to accept our students after doing their national examinations. We are certified by the Kenya National Educational Boards as an institution that offers quality education and standard examinations applicable nationally.

Creativity

Creativity plays a great role at a child's educational journey. It is a chance for the children to put in use the various disciplines taught but in a new and innovative way. We don't only encourage expression of creativity, but also showcase the beautiful talents that we nature.

Achievements

We have made great strides from the inception of the school to where we are right now. We began the school with a handful of students and now dozens of students do their national examinations at our school. Coming from a densely populated area of the capital We boast one of the highest teacher to student ratio not only in the capital but countrywide.
