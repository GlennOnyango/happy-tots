Feeding Program

Dandora is one of the slums that experiences urban hunger according to UHDSS 2018 report. In this case the school decided to start a feeding program to feed the children and keep them in school. The program has been running since the school inception. The kids are offered porridge at 10:00 am and Githeri 'Maize and beans' at lunch time. The benefits of the program are evident. Many of the pupils can't afford two meals per day and this factor makes the school a safe heaven for all the children who attend. We register a class attendance of almost 100% in all school days. Unfortunately with the numbers soaring every year, it is proving to be a challenge to sustain the population with the previous convectional means. To this end we are asking you to support us and keep a child at school by donating to our school.
